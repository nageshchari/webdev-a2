The Department of Computer Science and Technology at Goa University is one of the premier departments in the country,
offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in computer science and related fields.
The department was established in 1986 and has since been at the forefront of research and education in the field of computer science.

The department has a strong faculty team of professors and researchers who are actively involved in cutting-edge research
in areas such as artificial intelligence, data science, machine learning, computer networks, software engineering, and more.
The faculty regularly publish their research findings in top-tier conferences and journals.

The department has state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern computer labs equipped with the latest software and hardware.
The department also has a well-stocked library with a vast
collection of books, journals, and research papers in computer science and related fields.

The department has collaborations with several renowned institutions and industries in India and abroad,
which provides opportunities for students to work on real-world projects and gain practical experience.
The department also organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences on emerging topics in computer science, providing a platform for students to learn from experts in the field.

Overall, the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Goa University is committed to providing quality education and research opportunities to its students, preparing them for successful careers in the field of computer science and technology.
